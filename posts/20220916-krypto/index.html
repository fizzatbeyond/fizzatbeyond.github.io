Krypto

He has been with me through thick and thin. My ex and I got him when he was somewhere between 8 and 10 weeks old. That was over 12 years ago. He was there for me when we got divorced. He was there for me on those nights when I came home to an otherwise empty house. He was there for me when I started dating again and when I got married to my wonderful wife.

He always looks happy, and used to act that way too. This past year he has significantly slowed down. At this point he is on a high dose of pain medication. It’s hard to see him in so much pain every day. It has gotten to the point where he has a hard time getting up, especially after the medication has worn off. I have to regularly help him stand up because he can’t always do that on his own.

I can’t stand to see him suffer anymore. Today we are saying good bye to him.

Good bye old friend.
