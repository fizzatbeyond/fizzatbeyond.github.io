Mom

Today would have been my mom’s birthday, I believe her 67th one, but I am not sure about that. She passed away twelve years ago next month. Sometimes I still think about her and miss her but it has been a long time and a lot has happened. I am sure that my youngest son has no memory of her, and my oldest would have been 4 when she passed so I don’t think he remembers her either.

So I guess all I wanted to say was Happy Birthday Mom, I still miss you even if I don’t think about you all that often anymore.
